Information Architecture Driven

Clear hierarchy · Predictable · Low cognitive load
Design philosophy: Navigation should follow Fitts' Law and Hick's Law. Reduce choices per viewport. Mobile gets 4 tabs + AI FAB (floating action button). Desktop gets a unified search-anchored top nav. The AI assistant is promoted to a persistent FAB — highest discoverability, zero navigation cost.
Key decisions: (1) Notifications bell removed for buyers — moved to profile. (2) Search moves to all pages — embedded in navbar on desktop, modal on mobile. (3) Bottom bar capped at 4 items — "Orders" tucked under Profile tab.

🔑 Key design decisions:
  • Unified top bar: Same component on every page — dark green with search embedded.
  • Mobile: 4 tabs + AI FAB — Home, Products, Cart, Profile. Orders accessible from Profile. FAB = persistent AI entry, always visible, zero navigation cost.
